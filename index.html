Why We're Doing This

Our mission is born from a shared passion for leveraging technology to solve critical healthcare challenges. We believe that early detection is the key to preventing vision loss from treatable conditions, but access to screening remains a barrier for millions. DR.AI is our answer—a tool to make preliminary eye health screening accessible, affordable, and accurate for everyone, everywhere.

Expanding the Frontiers of Eye Health

Our research explores the use of AI to detect and classify a range of eye conditions from retinal images. By training our models on these categories, our goal is to support early, accessible, and accurate diagnosis to prevent vision loss.

Diabetic Retinopathy

A complication of diabetes where high blood sugar damages the blood vessels in the retina, which can lead to blindness if left untreated.

Age-Related Macular Degeneration (ARMD)

A disease that damages the macula—the central part of the retina—causing blurred or lost central vision, especially in older adults.

Cataracts

A clouding of the eye's natural lens, leading to blurry vision, glare sensitivity, and difficulty seeing at night. It is one of the most common causes of vision loss worldwide.

Glaucoma

A group of eye diseases that damage the optic nerve, often due to high eye pressure, and can result in permanent vision loss if not detected early.

Normal (Healthy)

Eyes that show no signs of disease or abnormalities, providing a baseline for AI models to learn and differentiate between healthy and diseased conditions.
